Tomato Basil Pasta

Servings: 2 | Prepping Time: 15 min | Cooking Time: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 200 grams spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and reserve 1 cup of the pasta water.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, then add diced tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook until sauce thickens.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to combine. Add reserved pasta water if needed.
  4. Off the heat, garnish with basil and Parmesan. Serve immediately.

Tips:

Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a kick. You can substitute spaghetti with any pasta of your choice.
